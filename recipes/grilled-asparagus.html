Grilled Asparagus

How to Grill Asparagus

This grilled asparagus recipe is simple

Let's you enjoy the natural flavor of fresh asparagus!

Fresh asparagus spears are coated with a bit of oil and salt and cooked quickly over high heat on the grill. That's it.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh asparagus, trimmed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat grill for high heat, lightly oil grate
  2. Lightly coat asparagus with olive oil
  3. Cook until lightly charred and fork-tender, turning often
  4. Season with salt to taste
